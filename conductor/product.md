# Product Guide - Eczanerede (Pharmacy Locator)

## Core Concept
Eczanerede is a mobile-first web application designed to help users in Turkey quickly find open and on-duty pharmacies. It focuses on providing a fast, reliable, and user-friendly experience during times when medication is needed urgently.

## Target Audience
- **Citizens in need of urgent medication:** Users looking for open pharmacies outside of regular business hours or in unfamiliar areas.
- **Mobile Users:** People on the go who need immediate location-based information and navigation.

## Primary Goals
- **Minimize Search Time:** Reduce the effort and time required to find an available pharmacy.
- **Accurate Navigation:** Provide precise, real-time turn-by-turn directions to the selected location.
- **Reliable Status Updates:** Ensure users are accurately informed whether a pharmacy is currently "open" or "on-duty".

## Key Features
- **Location-Based Discovery:** Automatically detects user location to show the nearest available pharmacies.
- **Real-Time Status Indicators:** Clear visual cues for "open" vs "on-duty" status based on scraped chamber data and regular working hours.
- **Mobile-Optimized UI:** A swipeable list interface (powered by Hammer.js) and responsive design (Tailwind CSS) tailored for quick mobile interaction.
- **Map Integration:** Interactive Google Maps view with markers and direction routing.
- **Automated Data Refresh:** Background scrapers for Eski≈üehir, Istanbul, and Ankara to keep duty information current.
- **Reliability Verified:** Backed by a comprehensive location testing suite covering 50+ scenarios across supported cities and edge cases.
